BABY SWIMMING

From the age of 6 months, infants can join a class accompanied by an adult.

  • A medical certificate for the infant from a pediatrician.
  • A medical certificate for the guardian from any medical specialist.
  • For the infant: a waterproof diaper, swimsuit, and bathrobe.
  • For the guardian: a swimsuit, non-slip slippers, a swimming cap, and a bathrobe.

Baby swimming classes last for 30 minutes.

You can make up for missed lessons if you notify the reception at least 3 hours before the class. The make-up lesson must be scheduled during an active subscription on a different day and time from your regular class. For more information, please refer to the operating regulations you receive upon registration.

You have the option to book a single day-pass lesson to get a full experience of baby swimming.

Contact our reception at 2331125087 to inquire about available spots. You can then register either by visiting us in person or by making a deposit to our swimming center's bank account.

Contact our reception at 2331125087 and book your spots by paying your subscription with cash, card, or a bank deposit. The subscription is valid from the date you declare as your first lesson, not from the payment date.

Each baby swimming class has 6 infants participating.

Babies, who lived and developed in their mother's womb for 9 months, make reflexive movements in the pool water. Through various playful exercises in the arms of their mom or dad and under the guidance of our coach, they unknowingly:

  • activate their muscles
  • strengthen their immune system
  • develop antibodies
  • become less susceptible to illnesses.
  • learn to control their bodies by changing positions and postures.
  • release their energy, resulting in calmer sleep.

Additionally, as a parent, you get to spend creative time with your child, developing stronger emotional bonds and discovering that your child will learn to swim in the simplest and most fun way, completely spontaneously with your participation. We don't teach your baby; you teach them lessons of love. At the same time, through the group, they develop their socialization skills.

In our changing rooms, the water automatically runs at 36°C, and there are changing tables, playpens, and hairdryers.

No weather conditions prevent the lessons. PEGASUS Aqua Center classes are held in a specially designed indoor pool. Both the pool and the surrounding area maintain a consistently warm temperature for the children. Therefore, lessons proceed as scheduled regardless of the weather.

Of course, you can watch the lesson from our canteen area.
